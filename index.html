About

Presenting Hobbes, a trailblazing cryptocurrency token inspired by Elon Musk's beloved Yorkshire Terrier, who accompanied him for an unforgettable 15 years.

Named after the philosopher Hobbes, this fierce little companion was dubbed "nasty, brutish, and short"—a nod to his tenacity and spirited nature. The Hobbes token embodies the essence of this extraordinary dog, channeling his bold energy into a powerful digital currency.

Designed to connect the worlds of cryptocurrency enthusiasts and dog lovers alike, Hobbes aims to foster a vibrant, resilient ecosystem, celebrating the unstoppable spirit of its canine namesake.

how to buy

create a wallet

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

get some eth

You will need ETH in your wallet to switch to $HOBBES. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to uniswap

Connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $HOBBES token address into Uniswap, select HOBBES, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
